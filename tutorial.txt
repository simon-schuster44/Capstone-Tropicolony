Tropicolony is a turn-based strategy game. Your main task is to build a little colony on an undiscovered tropical island.
You start with a deck of 12 cards. Each day, you can choose from 6 cards and after each day, new cards are drawn from your deck. 
When there are no more cards left in your deck, you can choose one out of three random cards from your deck.
